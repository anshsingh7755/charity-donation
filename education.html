Education Sub-Causes

Primary Education

Why It Matters: Primary education is the foundation for all future learning and development.

Our Work: We establish and support primary schools in underserved areas, providing quality education and learning materials.

Impact: Your donation provides school supplies, uniforms, mid-day meals, and quality instruction for underprivileged children ages 6-11.

Donate for Primary Education

Secondary Education

Why It Matters: Secondary education opens doors to higher opportunities and better career prospects.

Our Work: We support secondary schools with infrastructure, teachers, and scholarship programs for deserving students.

Impact: Your support helps provide quality education, digital learning tools, and vocational guidance for teenagers in need.

Donate for Secondary Education

Vocational Training

Why It Matters: Vocational skills enable youth to become economically independent and contribute to society.

Our Work: We run skill development centers teaching IT, tailoring, carpentry, and other in-demand trades.

Impact: Your contribution funds training programs, tools, and job placement assistance for underprivileged youth.

Donate for Vocational Training
