Shelter Sub-Causes

Emergency Shelter

Why It Matters: During disasters and crises, emergency shelter saves lives and provides immediate relief.

Our Work: We set up emergency shelters after natural disasters, providing temporary safe housing and essentials.

Impact: Your donation provides tents, blankets, food, water, and medical aid to displaced families in crisis situations.

Donate for Emergency Shelter

Permanent Housing

Why It Matters: A permanent home provides stability and dignity for homeless families to rebuild their lives.

Our Work: We build affordable housing communities for homeless and low-income families in urban and rural areas.

Impact: Your support constructs homes with basic utilities, ensuring families have a secure place to call their own.

Donate for Permanent Housing

Rehabilitation Centers

Why It Matters: Rehabilitation centers help vulnerable people transition from streets to self-sufficient living.

Our Work: We operate rehabilitation centers providing shelter, counseling, job training, and life skills development.

Impact: Your contribution supports facilities, staff, counseling services, and skill training for rehabilitation residents.

Donate for Rehabilitation
